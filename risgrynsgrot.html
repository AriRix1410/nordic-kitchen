Risgrynsgröt

Ingredients

  • ¾ Cup Long Grain Rice
  • 6 Cups Boiling Water
  • 1 Quart Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter (Melted)
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • ½ Teaspoon Almond Extract
  • Freshly Ground Nutmeg
  • ½ Cup Ground Blanched Almond

Method

  1. Place the rice in a strainer and pour the boiling water over the rice. Drain well.
  2. Place the rice, milk and salt in a double boiler and cook, covered, for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until all is thick and creamy.
  3. Stir in the butter, sugar, almond extract, nutmeg and almonds.
  4. Serve warm or chilled.
