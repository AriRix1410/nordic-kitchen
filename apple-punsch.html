Apple Punsch

Ingredients

  • 4 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  • ½ Teaspoon Allspice
  • 1 Teaspoon Whole Cloves
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • ¼ Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Pinch Ground Nutmeg
  • 1 Orange (Large, Quarted with Peel)
  • 2 Litres Apple Juice

Method

  1. Place filter in coffee maker basket.
  2. Fill with brown sugar, allspice, cloves, cinnamon stick, salt, nutmeg and orange wedges.
  3. Pour apple juice into coffee pot where the water usually goes.
  4. Brew, and serve hot.
